About

I'm Josh Brandon — a 3D character artist and game developer based in Australia. I run Lil Indie Games, where I design, develop, and ship games from the ground up.

From concept to final render, I work across the full art pipeline: character modeling, hand-painted textures, concept art, 2D illustration, and game development in Unity. I like making things that feel alive and have personality.

Open to studio roles, freelance commissions, and collaborations. If something here catches your eye, get in touch.
