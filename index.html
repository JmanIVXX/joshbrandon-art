About

I'm Josh Brandon — a 3D game artist and developer based in Australia. I run Lil Indie Games, where I design, develop, and ship games from the ground up.

From concept to final render, I work across the full art pipeline: hand-painted 3D environments, character design, concept art, 2D illustration, and game development in Unity. I like making things that feel alive and have personality.

Open to studio roles, freelance commissions, and collaborations. If something here catches your eye, get in touch.
